📊 FPT Financial Growth Analysis (2002–2024)
📌 Overview

This project analyzes the 20-year financial development of FPT Corporation from 2002 to 2024. The goal is to understand how the company evolved through different economic phases, identify key growth drivers, and provide strategic insights based on historical data.

The analysis focuses on three main financial indicators:

Total Assets
Net Revenue
Net Profit After Tax
🎯 Objectives
Evaluate year-over-year (YoY) growth trends of key financial metrics
Analyze long-term growth using CAGR
Identify major turning points (IPO, crisis, restructuring, digital transformation)
Generate insights and strategic recommendations
📂 Data Source
FPT Annual Reports
Consolidated Financial Statements
Time range: 2002 – 2024
📊 Key Findings
1. Strong and Sustainable Growth
Profit CAGR (~31.8%) > Assets (~24.8%) > Revenue (~18.5%)
→ Indicates improving operational efficiency and profit margins over time
2. High Volatility Periods
Large fluctuations in assets and revenue due to:
IPO (2006–2007)
Global Financial Crisis (2008)
Restructuring & Digital Pivot (2018)
3. Transformation Strategy

FPT evolved through 3 major phases:

2002–2005: Foundation & domestic expansion
2006–2015: Diversification & stabilization
2016–2024: Digital transformation (AI, Cloud, Global expansion)
📈 Financial Trends
Growth Patterns
Early stage: explosive growth (startup → scaling phase)
Mid stage: stable expansion with controlled risk
Recent stage: high-margin growth driven by digital services
Profitability
Net margin increased from ~2% → ~10%+
Shift toward high-value services (AI, Cloud, SaaS)
Capital Structure
Debt-to-Equity reduced significantly (from ~15x → ~1x)
→ Stronger financial stability
⚠️ Risks Identified
Fluctuating gross margins during crisis periods
Liquidity pressure (Current Ratio sometimes < 1.0)
Increasing competition in global AI & Cloud markets
🚀 Opportunities
Expansion into international markets
Growth of AI, SaaS, and digital transformation services
Strong positioning in Vietnam and ASEAN tech ecosystem
💡 Recommendations
Optimize cost structure and capital allocation (CAPEX control)
Focus on high-margin segments (AI, Cloud, SaaS)
Maintain financial balance:
Debt/Equity ≤ 1
Current Ratio ≥ 1
Improve forecasting using rolling-window analysis
🧠 Conclusion

FPT demonstrates strong long-term growth, high adaptability, and a successful transition into a technology-driven enterprise. Despite some risks, its financial health and strategic direction position it well for continued expansion in the global digital economy.
